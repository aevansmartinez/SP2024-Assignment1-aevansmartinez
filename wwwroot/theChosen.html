The Chosen

IMDb Rating: 9.2/10

View on IMDb

The Chosen is a television drama based on the life of Jesus Christ, created, directed, and co-written by American filmmaker Dallas Jenkins. It is the first multi-season series about the life of Christ, and season one was the highest crowd-funded TV series or film project of all time. The show portrays Jesus "through the eyes of those who met him".
