Downton Abbey is a British historical drama television series created by Julian Fellowes. Set in the fictional Yorkshire country estate of Downton Abbey between 1912 and 1926, the series depicts the lives of the aristocratic Crawley family and their domestic servants. It explores themes of social hierarchy, class, and change in early 20th century England.
